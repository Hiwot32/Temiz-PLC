About Us

At Temiz PLC, we are dedicated to delivering premium-quality textiles that celebrate Ethiopia’s artistry and creativity. By combining modern design with traditional techniques, we create fabrics that stand out in both fashion and interior design markets.
Our mission is to support local artisans, innovate in textile production, and provide customers with sustainable and stylish fabric solutions.
